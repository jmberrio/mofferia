Game rules

  1. The game target is pickung up as many "feria" light bulbs as possible and dropping them off in the main gate.
  2. You have {{tiempo}} seconds to collect as many as you can.
  3. When you pick a lantern, it'll be added to you queue and you'll drag them along the field.
  4. You need at least {{numMinimo}} lanterns to deliver them to the main gate. When you get {{numMinimo}} they'll change color so that you know you can drop them off
  5. If you run into an enemy, you'll lose all the lanterns you're dragging at the moment and {{penalizacion}} seconds will deducted from the total time you have left.
  6. Once the time is up, the score will be the amount of lanterns you were able to bring to the main gate.

Movements

  1. The girl moves by herself through the feria, unless she crashes against a "caseta" or the limit of the grounds.
  2. You'll start from the Main gate, towards North, but you can change directions with your keyboard arrows.
  3. But don't try to do strange things: if you're going right, you cannot directly move left, you'll first need to move up or down. 90ยบ angles only.

Enemies

  1. Drunk people and riders are moving along the whole feria without caring. Be careful and don't run into them!!.
  2. If you do, you'll lose your bounty, so don't accumulate too many lanterns without dropping them off.

Turns

  • Each player will play their own match.
